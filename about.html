About Us

Leader In Business Innovation

On 26th March 2008, we as a dynamic team of 2 people joined forces to co-found JS Assignments Private Ltd. We are now 2 directors and 1 technical director of the company who are professionals Chartered Accountants, MBA and Engineers in varied fields. Our combined knowledge and experience allowed us to launch and grow our company in competitive industries.

We are committed for “You Assign, We Deliver”. It is prevalent from our turnover which rose from Rs. 2.55Cr in 2017-2018 to Rs. 10.30Cr. in 2021-2022 & Rs. 12.70Cr. We divide our efforts into three categories. We serve as leading business units:

  • Infrastructure Development
  • E-solution providers for E-Mudra and TIN FC of NSDL
  • Management consultants for existing & new set-ups, Retainers and Compliances
  • Risk Management Services

On Infrastructure Development projects, we work as a lead subcontractor and manage the bulk of the work for top National and International contractors. Earthwork & Road works are two of our specialties in the construction industry. Our infrastructure projects range in size from municipal transportation and excavation & construction of Expressways, National Highways, Railways and Bullet Train Projects. We have expertise in:

  • Earthwork Projects
  • GSB
  • WMM
  • DBM

We combine all of our real-world expertise to offer huge corporations and business houses Management Consulting Services. As professionals and experienced management, we offer accounting, tax planning, and consulting services. By doing risk management analysis, company valuation, and management consulting, we act as a business counsellor to many groups and assist them in setting up and structuring the firm.

Our consulting business includes assisting government projects heavily. We have carried out a number of government projects for educational institutions and assisted a number of businesses in preparing and successfully submitting an electronic tender. Our clients put their trust in us to perform their business valuation so they can restructure their businesses financially. By putting our fund-raising model into practise through our initiative, we have aided numerous projects. As management consultants, we have expertise in:

  • Accounting outsourcing work – Domestic and Global
  • High Net worth Individual Tax planning and Consultancy – Direct and Indirect Taxes
  • Business Advisory
  • Business Structuring
  • Risk Management and Analysis – insurance consultants ▪ Business Valuation and immovable property valuations
  • Business Valuation – Corporate Consultancy
  • Financial Structuring, Project Reports Preparation – Execution, Fund Raising model implementation (Equity, Debt, Hybrid, others…)
  • Others - Execution of Government projects for Educational Institutes

The other category of projects we accept has to do with Franchise and Dealership Management. We have our own channel network through which we are offering E-Mudra’s Sub LRA – TIN FC the finest support available. We are the best e-solutions team. Our clientele returns to us because of the quality of our services which is mainly for compliance services for Direct and Indirect Taxes along with retainership services for book keeping and record keeping services.

We are growing our company model and have a long list of triumphs. We commit to grow our own business verticals and also clients' businesses to new heights using our experience.
