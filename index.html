About Us

Leader In Business Innovation

JS Assignments Private Limited (JS Assignments) based in Gujarat, India a vibrant team of all promoters - entrepreneurs who are all Chartered Accountants and other professionals was established on 26th Mar 2008 in rendering the professional services in various verticals of business. Team of JS Assignments is mainly working in the areas of infrastructure development related to roads, canals, railways, etc. for earthwork projects and has been continuously rendering professional services in the field of Accounting, Taxation, Finance, Insurance, IT enabled and Web based Services and Academics since inception.

Our professional team of JS Assignments has expanded new horizons of rendering services in the field of Financial Advisory services along with handling the Government projects on behalf of Clients. We have grown not only in terms of the number of clients but also grown in terms of geographical reach to clients across India. JS Assignments has more than 300 clients including leading infrastructure companies like L&T, ICA and other Banks, Manufacturing & Service sector companies, Government offices, big branded Educational companies/institutes and NGOs across India.

Management Consultancy

As professionals and experienced management, we offer accounting, tax planning, and consulting services. By doing risk management analysis, company valuation, and management consulting, we act as a business counsellor to many groups and assist them in setting up and structuring the firm.

Infrastructure Development

On infrastructure development projects, we work as a lead subcontractor and manage the bulk of the work for top National and International contractors. Earthwork & Road works are two of our specialties in the construction industry. Our infrastructure projects range in size from municipal transportation and excavation & construction of Expressways, National Highways, Railways and Bullet Train Projects.
